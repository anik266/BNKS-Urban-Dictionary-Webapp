About BNKS Urban Dictionary
Hello world!

BNKS Urban Dictionary is a small-scale web app side project that I did over my Spring Break. While working on my CS142 final project in college, I was bored by how little the return on investment was. I must have written 2000 lines of code for its final project and yet after the finals was over, I knew there would be nothing left of it. Localhost is, after all, just local.

So I decided to use some of my code from that project and build an entirely different thing that I can perhaps launch and have it up there on the internet (forever?). I am not sure why the idea of Urban Dictionary for BNKS, my alma mater, was interesting enough to me. But once I began working on it, I realized that it IS interesting! Looking backwards, I think it was the idea that kept me going, not the code. Even if this app is simple, it threw many new challenges at me and I had a great time trying to piece things together.

My goal was to build something from scratch -- anything that is interesting -- interesting enough to keep me hooked so that I would not get bored midway and hopefully lead the project to completion. If you're reading this, it means that this project is working correctly and I have hence achieved what I sought.
